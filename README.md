# ✈️ Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis Dashboard** is an end-to-end Power BI project designed to analyze customer satisfaction, travel behavior, service quality, and operational performance using feedback from **129,000+ airline passengers**.

The dashboard helps identify key drivers of customer satisfaction, understand passenger demographics and travel patterns, evaluate service performance, and uncover operational issues impacting customer experience.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Airlines operate in a highly competitive environment where customer experience directly impacts loyalty, retention, and profitability.

This analysis aims to answer critical business questions:

- What percentage of airline passengers are satisfied?
- Does satisfaction vary by customer type, travel purpose, or travel class?
- What is the profile of a loyal airline passenger?
- How does flight distance influence travel patterns and customer preferences?
- Which onboard and airport services contribute most to customer satisfaction?
- How do delays affect passenger experience and retention?

# 📈 Key Business Insights

### 👥 Customer Insights

- More than **4 out of every 5 passengers** are returning customers
- Business travelers demonstrate higher satisfaction levels than personal travelers
- Business Class passengers exhibit stronger loyalty and retention rates

### ⭐ Service Insights

- Online Boarding, Seat Comfort, and Entertainment are major satisfaction drivers
- Passengers consistently rate Cleanliness and Baggage Handling positively
- Wi-Fi and Food Services present improvement opportunities

### ⚙️ Operational Insights

- Flight delays are strongly associated with reduced satisfaction
- Satisfaction levels decline significantly for heavily delayed flights
- Operational reliability remains a key retention factor
