# ✈️ Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis Dashboard** is an end-to-end Power BI analytics project designed to analyze passenger satisfaction, travel behavior, service quality, and airline operational performance using feedback from over **129,000 airline passengers**.

The dashboard transforms passenger survey responses and flight-related information into actionable insights that help stakeholders improve customer experience, increase retention, optimize service delivery, and reduce operational inefficiencies.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem Statement

Customer experience is one of the most important competitive advantages in the airline industry. Passenger satisfaction directly impacts:

- Customer retention
- Brand loyalty
- Repeat bookings
- Revenue growth
- Airline reputation

Airlines need to understand:

- Which passengers are most satisfied?
- What characteristics define loyal customers?
- Which services contribute most to satisfaction?
- How operational delays impact customer experience?
- Which service areas require immediate improvement?

This dashboard addresses these questions through a centralized analytical platform.

# 📈 Key Business Outcomes

### 👥 Customer Insights

- Loyal customers demonstrate significantly higher satisfaction levels.
- Business travelers contribute strongly to retention performance.
- Travel class plays a major role in customer experience.

### ⭐ Service Insights

- Online Boarding, Seat Comfort, and Entertainment are primary satisfaction drivers.
- Wi-Fi and Food Services represent improvement opportunities.
- Service quality has a stronger impact on satisfaction than demographic factors.

### ⚙️ Operational Insights

- Delays are strongly associated with dissatisfaction.
- Reliable operations support customer loyalty.
- Improving operational efficiency can directly improve passenger satisfaction.
